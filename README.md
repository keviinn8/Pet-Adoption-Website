# Pet-Adoption-Website
## A website named "Adopt A-Cat/Dog Adopt Your Bestie" to find dogs and cats to adopt, or to put your own up for adoption.

This website is designed for pet lovers who want to adopt or give away dogs and cats. Users can browse available pets by filling out a form based on their preferences. To put a pet up for adoption, users must first create an account or log in, then complete a form with the petâ€™s details. The site also offers helpful information on how to care for dogs and cats, making it a valuable resource for both new and experienced pet owners.
